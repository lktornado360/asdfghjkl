Apps!

Directions

Click a game to download it.

In the Tank Time Windows games, use the arrow keys to control the green tank and space to shoot from it. In Tank Time 1 & 2, use WASD to control the red tank and z, x, c, or v to shoot.

In the Tank Time mobile games, use your finger to control the red dude. Sadly, due to a glitch I have yet to fix, you can only have one finger on the screen at a time.

In the Leaf Boy games, tap to shoot MAN★MAN or Sam Hashtag with your laser (or don't--ouch!). In Leaf Boy 2, grab a friend to use the arrow keys to control the good guys.

In the MAN★MAN vs. Jinx-A-Saurus games, press space to shoot MAN★MAN with your fireballs. In MAN★MAN vs. Jinx-A-Saurus 2, grab a friend to use the arrow keys to control MAN★MAN.

In KatBikerz, press space to go and the arrow keys to turn. You are the cat. Try to turn the dog on his head to put him behind.

In Digital Shark Attack, tap to jump. It's a work in progress.
